The Full Body Power Routine: 5-Day Workout Plan

Day 1: Upper Body Blast

Bench Press:       3 sets of 8 reps

Pull-Ups or Rows:     3 sets of 10 reps
Overhead Shoulder Press:     3 sets of 8 reps
Bicep Curls:     3 sets of 12 reps
Tricep Dips:     3 sets of 10 reps


Day 2: Lower Body Sculpt

Squats:     4 sets of 10 reps
Deadlifts:     3 sets of 8 reps
Lunges:     3 sets of 12 reps (each leg)
Calf Raises:     4 sets of 15 reps


Day 3: Active Recovery
30 minutes of light cardio (jogging, cycling, or swimming)
Stretching and mobility exercises

Day 4: Core Crusher
Planks:     4 sets of 1-minute holds
Russian Twists:     3 sets of 15 reps (each side)
Leg Raises:     3 sets of 12 reps
Bicycle Crunches:     3 sets of 20 reps


Day 5: Total Body Burn
Clean and Press:     3 sets of 6 reps
Bent-Over Rows:     3 sets of 10 reps
Kettlebell Swings:     4 sets of 15 reps
Push-Ups:     3 sets of 12 reps
Mountain Climbers:     3 sets of 20 reps


Pro Tips for Success: Progressive Overload:
Gradually increase weights or resistance to challenge your muscles.

Rest and Recovery:
Allow at least 48 hours of rest between intense workouts.
Nutrition Matters:
Drink plenty of water throughout the day.
Stay Hydrated:
Consume a balanced diet rich in protein, carbs, and healthy fats to fuel your workouts and aid recovery.
